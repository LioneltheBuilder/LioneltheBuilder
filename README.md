## 🔨 What I’m Building

### The Digital Builders

A creative technology company helping entrepreneurs, artists, nonprofits, and community organizations transform ideas into working digital products.

Our work includes:

* Full-stack web applications and MVP development
* AI-assisted business and creative workflows
* Product strategy, prototyping, and systems architecture
* Community platforms and event technology
* Technical education, workshops, and builder mentorship
* Launch stabilization, quality assurance, and documentation

### TOTL Agency

A curated talent discovery, application, and booking platform built for models, actors, creators, Career Builders, and casting teams.

TOTL is designed around a clear principle:

> **Not a marketplace. An agency.**

The platform combines professional talent profiles, opportunity management, structured applications, bookings, communication, and administrative oversight.

**Core technology:** Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase, PostgreSQL, Vercel, Stripe, automated testing, and GitHub-based development workflows.

[Visit TOTL Agency](https://totl.digitalbuilders757.com) · [View the repository](https://github.com/Digital-Builders-757/totl)

### VizBVA

A community-centered event and culture platform I co-founded to help people discover meaningful experiences, organizers, businesses, and creative communities across Hampton Roads.

My work includes product development, launch strategy, community partnerships, organizer outreach, and the systems supporting event discovery.

## 🧭 How I Work

I work across the full product lifecycle:

```text
Idea
  ↓
Discovery and product strategy
  ↓
Prototype and systems design
  ↓
Database and application architecture
  ↓
Implementation
  ↓
Testing and launch readiness
  ↓
Documentation and iteration
```

I increasingly use AI coding agents as part of structured engineering workflows—not as replacements for judgment, but as collaborators operating inside clear plans, tests, documentation, and review boundaries.

My development process commonly includes:

* Issue-driven planning
* Feature branches and pull requests
* Database migrations and rollback planning
* Automated and manual QA
* Playwright end-to-end testing
* GitHub Actions and deployment checks
* Architecture and operational documentation
* Staging, preview, and production environments
